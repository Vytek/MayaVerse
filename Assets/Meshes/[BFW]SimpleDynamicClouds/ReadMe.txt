Assets Name: [BFW]Simple Dynamic Clouds

Version: V1.0

Publisher: Butterfly World

E-Mail: srovictor@qq.com

direction：

	1.Just put the prefab(Assets\2DClouds\Prefab\Clouds.prefab) into your scene.

	2.Shader has three types of textures: ScatterMap, DensityMap, TextureMap, in "Assets\2DClouds\Textures" folder.

	3.Shader property "Color" control cloud color and opacity.


Features:
	- Support HDR.
	- Good effect on Mobile(high performance).
	- Simple and easy to use.
	- Support Unity4 & Unity5, Free & Pro.
